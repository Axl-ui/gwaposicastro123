San Remigio Cove Beach Resort

San Remigio Cove Beach Resort, formerly known as Samalan Camp and Sea, is a tranquil beachfront destination located in Barangay San Remigio, Kaputian District, Island Garden City of Samal, Davao del Norte. The resort features a 300-meter stretch of white sand beach, ideal for swimming and relaxation. Guests can choose from various accommodations, including air-conditioned rooms, fan-cooled nipa huts, and dormitory-style units. For those seeking a more immersive experience, the resort offers beachfront camping grounds. Amenities include a barbecue area, washrooms, and a function hall for events. The resort's serene atmosphere makes it a perfect getaway for families and groups looking to enjoy the natural beauty of Samal Island.

Morata Hiker's Hub

Morata Hiker's Hub, located in Davao City, Philippines, is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers. It serves as a starting point for hikers exploring nearby mountains and trails, offering an accessible base camp for those aiming to summit local peaks. The hub is designed to provide services and amenities for hikers, such as rest areas, basic facilities, and guidance for trekking adventures. With its scenic views and proximity to nature, Morata Hiker's Hub is a favorite stop for individuals or groups looking to experience Davao's natural beauty and embark on outdoor expeditions.

Darrporrt

Darrport, located in Davao del Sur, Philippines, is a lesser-known yet picturesque tourist spot that offers a tranquil escape for visitors looking to enjoy nature's beauty. The area is popular for its serene ambiance, lush greenery, and scenic views, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a peaceful retreat. Darrport is also known for its proximity to hiking trails, waterfalls, and natural attractions, providing a perfect setting for outdoor activities such as trekking, sightseeing, and nature photography. It is a hidden gem for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
