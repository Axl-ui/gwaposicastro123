Panas Falls

Panas Falls, located in the municipality of Don Marcelino, Davao Occidental, Philippines, is a beautiful natural attraction known for its stunning multi-tiered waterfalls and crystal-clear waters. The falls are set in a lush, green rainforest, offering a serene and scenic environment for visitors. It is a popular spot for eco-tourism, where tourists can enjoy activities like swimming in the cool pools, picnicking by the falls, and exploring the surrounding nature trails. Panas Falls is a must-visit for those looking to experience Davao's pristine natural beauty and enjoy an adventure in the great outdoors.

Epol Falls

Epol Falls, located in the municipality of Bansalan, Davao del Sur, Philippines, is a captivating natural attraction known for its scenic beauty and refreshing waters. The falls are set amidst lush greenery, creating a serene and tranquil atmosphere perfect for relaxation and nature exploration. The waterfall cascades down a rocky cliff into a natural pool, making it an ideal spot for swimming, picnicking, and photography. Epol Falls is also surrounded by hiking trails, allowing visitors to enjoy a scenic trek while immersing themselves in the region's rich biodiversity. It is a popular destination for eco-tourism and outdoor enthusiasts.

Danlugan Falls

Danlugan Falls is located in Davao City, Philippines. It is a popular tourist spot known for its picturesque beauty, with clear, cascading waters surrounded by lush forests. The falls is a great destination for nature lovers, offering opportunities for hiking, swimming, and relaxing in a serene, natural setting. It's a hidden gem within Davao, ideal for those looking to explore the outdoors and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of the area.
